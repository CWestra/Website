Cabbage rolls

What you will need:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp poultry seasoning
  • 2 tsp salt & pepper
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1 lb of ground pork
  • 1 large head of savoy cabbage
  • 2 8oz cans of hunt's tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1 litre saurkraut
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • toothpicks

Directions:

  1. Boil your head of cabbage and peel off leafs as they cook and set aside in a strainer to get rid of excess water and let cool
  2. Mix together your ground meats, diced onions, cooked rice, eggs, extra virgin olive oil, garlic and poultry seasoning until well combined
  3. After your cabbage has cooled and your stuffing is ready you can start filling and rolling your cabbage and then binding them closed with a toothpick or two then placing inside of your baking dish
  4. For your simple sauce combine your tomato sauce brown sugar and saurkraut with its juice mix it all together and pour over the cabbage rolls bake at 350 degrees farenheight for 2 hours basting at 30minute intervals
  5. Serve and enjoy! dont forget to take your toothpicks out!
