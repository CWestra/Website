Chocolate Bailey's Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz of semi sweet baker's chocolate (not chocolate chips)
  • 1/3 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Bailey's irish cream liqueur
  • 12 oz room temprature cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 4 tbsp melted unsalted butter
  • 6 grams graham wafers or chocolate cookie crumbles
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar

Directions:

  1. Get a springform pan and line it with parchment and or grease it with butter
  2. blitz together your decided cookie or cracker with your melted butter and 1 tbsp of sugar once combined press firmly into the bottom of your greased springform pan
  3. Get a pot of simmering water started (not boiling) and a bowl that fits in the top so it doesnt touch the water, put the chocolate in the bowl and stir occasionally until melted, once melted take off element but keep on pot to stay tempered
  4. In an electric mixer add your 1/3 cup sugar, 2 cups heavy cream, vanilla extract and bailey's and mix on medium-high till siff peaks then set aside
  5. In another bowl beat your cream cheese until smooth then add your melted chocolate continuously mixing till fully incorporated then add your whipped cream till it's all combined then pour into your spring form pan to chill in the fridge for a day or freezer if you need it to set sooner
  6. Optional: Chocolate shavings, whipped cream, or berries ontop when it is ready to cut and serve
