Cream of mushroom soup

What you will need:

  • 1 large chopped onion
  • 6 Cloves of minced garlic
  • 5 Cups sliced brown mushrooms
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of fresh or dried thyme
  • 2 Tablespoons of butter
  • 1 Table spoon of olive oil
  • 3 Tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 Cup of chicken stock or broth
  • 1 Cup of half and half
  • 1 Tablespoon Worchestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Add your butter and olive oil into a large heavy saucepan and set the element to med-high and add your onions and cook till translucent then add your sliced mushrooms garlic and cook till all mushrooms are browned
  2. Add your flour to your onions garlic mushroom and fat mixture to make a roux making sure to continuously mix and cook for 2 minutes add worchestershire sauce wine stock bay leaf and thyme then simmer for 15 minutes
  3. turn your heat on low and add the half and half and let sit till warm take your bay leaf out at the end
  4. Optional: Chopped chives, Parsley or green onions for garnish ontop
  5. Serve and enjoy
