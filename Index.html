Cream of mushroom soup

What you will need:

  • 1 large chopped onion
  • 6 Cloves of minced garlic
  • 5 Cups sliced brown mushrooms
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of fresh or dried thyme
  • 2 Tablespoons of butter
  • 1 Table spoon of olive oil
  • 3 Tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 Cup of chicken stock or broth
  • 1 Cup of half and half
  • 1 Tablespoon Worchestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Add your butter and olive oil into a large heavy saucepan and set the element to med-high and add your onions and cook till translucent then add your garlic and cook for about a minute
  2. Add your flour to your onions garlic and fat mixture to make a roux making sure to continuously mix it around so it doesnt burn once fully incorperated add small increments of your chicken stock whisking so it doesnt clump
  3. In another pan sautee your sliced mushrooms
  4. Combine all of the rest of the ingredients to your saucepan and simmer for about 15 minutes and be sure to stir occasionaly and take your bay leaf out at the end
  5. Optional: Chopped chives, Parsley or green onions for garnish ontop
  6. Serve and enjoy
